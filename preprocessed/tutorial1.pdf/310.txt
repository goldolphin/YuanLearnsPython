【PG】You can think of the screen (or each line) as being laid out in blocks of eight spaces. Notice 
【PG】that, as the ABC sequence gets longer, the XYZ stays in the same place. The \t tells Python to 
【PG】start the XYZ at the next tab stop, or at the next available block. But once the ABC sequence 
【PG】gets big enough to fill the first block, Python moves the XYZ over to the next tab stop.
